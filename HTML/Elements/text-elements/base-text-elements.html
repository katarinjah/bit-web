The Giving Tree by Shell Silverstein

First subtitle

Once there was a tree... And she loved a little boy. And every day the boy would come and he would gather her leaves and make them into crowns and play king of the forest.

Second subtitle

He would climb up her trunk and swing from her branches and eat apples.

Third subtitle

And they would play hide-and-go-seek.And when he was tired, he would sleep in her shade. And the boy loved the tree... very much.
